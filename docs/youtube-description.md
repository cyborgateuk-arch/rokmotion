🎯 3 WAYS TO MAKE VIDEOS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

① USER VOICEOVER
   Upload your own audio → Grok builds visuals synced to your voice.

② ELEVENLABS + SCRIPT
   Grok writes a script → ElevenLabs generates narration → scenes auto-sync to speech.

③ VIDEO TO VIDEO
   Upload existing footage → keep original audio → add animated overlays.
   Show your video in a box or circle during overlays, or hide it entirely.

💬 WHAT IS ROKMOTION?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Rokmotion is an open-source project that combines Remotion (create videos with React code) and Grok (AI that writes the code for you). Tell Grok what video you want — a tutorial, promo, paper animation, or edited clip — and it scaffolds the composition, syncs audio, and renders an MP4.

Perfect for developers, creators, and anyone who wants precise motion graphics without manual video editing.
